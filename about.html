About

The Image sorter was originally developed for Brooklyn Law School. BLS hosts many lectures and conventions. These are photographed by people multiple different within the school, and by outside guests.

However, because each department has it's own stash of images. Whenever an employee is looking for a specefic shot they'll have to contact 4-5 people and frequently the same requests will be made multiple times.

The image sorter, allows employees to upload images to a shared google drive folder and assign the images specefic tags. So, if a BLS employee needs to picture of the Dean with students and professors, they can search those tags and immediately see every one of those images that is available.
